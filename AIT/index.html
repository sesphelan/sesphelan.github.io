Description:

From the bottom up, I created an Express application consisting of multiple user-interaction features, such as
a playable JavaScript version of Snake, a section for Reviews/Comments for myself to read, and a High Scores Leaderboard.
In order to record your score, please make an account and choose a difficulty to begin playing the game.

The data recorded by users is managed by MongoDB. The site was built with Node JS.
